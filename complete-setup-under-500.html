Assembly Timeline & Setup Guide

Day 1: Foundation (3 hours)

  • Morning: Assemble chair (45-60 minutes)
  • Afternoon: Set up desk (30 minutes)
  • Evening: Install basic lighting (45 minutes)

Day 2: Optimization (2 hours)

  • Morning: Install monitor arm and cable management (60 minutes)
  • Afternoon: Set up accessories and organize (60 minutes)

Day 3: Fine-Tuning (1 hour)

  • Adjust chair: Proper height, lumbar, armrests
  • Position monitor: Top at eye level, 20-30 inches away
  • Test lighting: No glare on screens, even illumination

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really get a quality setup for $500?

Absolutely. This isn't luxury, but it's professional-grade equipment that will last 3-5 years with daily use. The key is allocating budget to the right areas (chair first) and avoiding overpriced "gaming" gear.

What if I already have some items?

Reallocate the savings to better versions of other components. Already have a decent desk? Upgrade to the BenQ desk lamp ($99) or invest in a higher-quality chair.

How does this compare to a $1000 setup?

You get 80% of the comfort and functionality for 50% of the cost. The extra $500 buys longer warranties, premium materials, and brand names - not dramatically better ergonomics.

What's the first thing I should upgrade later?

The chair. If you eventually can spend another $200-300, upgrading to a used Herman Miller or Steelcase chair will provide the single biggest improvement to your setup.
