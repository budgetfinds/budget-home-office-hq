Part 1: Choosing Your Desk

Option A: Standard Desk (Best for most people)

Top Pick: Flexispot EC1 Desk - $129

55" wide, 24" deep - perfect for dual monitors. The steel frame supports 110 lbs, and the laminated surface resists coffee stains better than most $300 desks.

What to Look For:

  • Width: Minimum 48" for comfortable dual-screen setup
  • Depth: 24"-30" for proper monitor distance
  • Height: 29"-30" standard (check your chair height)
  • Material: Laminate over particle board (avoid MDF)

Part 3: Cable Management Solutions

The $25 Cable Management Kit

Essential Items:

  • Cable Sleeve ($12): Neoprene tube that bundles all cables
