Small Space Layout Ideas

Closet Office Conversion

Requirements:

  • Minimum 24" deep closet
  • Remove doors or use bifold doors
  • Add lighting (LED strip works well)
  • Use vertical space for storage

Advantages:

  • Completely hidden when not in use
  • Minimal space requirement
  • Built-in shelving potential

Room Divider Office

Requirements:

  • Bookshelf or room divider screen
  • Small desk against divider
  • Facing away from living space

Advantages:

  • Creates visual separation
  • Uses existing furniture
  • Maintains open feel

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the absolute minimum space needed?

18 square feet: 36" wide desk (9 sq ft) + 36" clearance for chair (9 sq ft). This is tight but functional for short work sessions.

How can I make a small space feel larger?

Use light colors, mirrors, and transparent materials. Keep cables hidden and surfaces clear. Good lighting also makes spaces feel larger.

Are standing desks good for small spaces?

Yes, but choose standing desk converters rather than full standing desks. Converters sit on existing surfaces and take much less space.

How do I handle video calls in small shared spaces?

Use a room divider or backdrop. Position your camera to show only your area. Use headphones with good noise cancellation.
