Why This Allocation Works

Chair: 35-40% of Budget

Your chair is the most important investment. Poor chairs cause back pain, reduce productivity, and cost more in medical bills than a good chair costs. The sweet spot for value is $150-$250.

Desk: 25-30% of Budget

Your desk needs to be the right height, stable, and large enough for your work. Standing desk converters provide the most value for money if you already have a table.

Lighting: 15-20% of Budget

Proper lighting reduces eye strain, headaches, and makes you look professional on video calls. This is often the most overlooked area in budget setups.

Accessories: 15-20% of Budget

Monitor arms, cable management, and organizers dramatically improve functionality and professional appearance. Don't skip these to save small amounts.
