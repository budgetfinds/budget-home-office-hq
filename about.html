About Budget Home Office HQ

Helping you build professional workspaces without professional prices

We're a team of ergonomics enthusiasts, former office managers, and value-seeking professionals who believe everyone deserves a comfortable, productive workspace - regardless of budget.

Our Story

In 2020, like millions of others, we suddenly found ourselves working from home. Kitchen tables became desks, dining chairs became office chairs, and poor lighting became daily headaches.

We quickly discovered that most "home office advice" was either:

  • Unrealistically expensive ("just buy a $1,200 Herman Miller chair")
  • Dangerously cheap ("this $50 chair will solve all your problems")
  • Written by people who hadn't actually tested the products

So we decided to do something about it.

Our Testing Methodology

Real-World Testing

We don't just unbox products and take pretty pictures. Every product we recommend goes through:

Test Phase Duration What We Evaluate
Initial Assembly 1-2 hours Ease of setup, instructions clarity, tool requirements
Daily Use 30-60 days Comfort, durability, adjustments, real-world performance
Stress Testing 2-4 weeks Weight capacity, material durability, mechanism reliability
Long-Term Follow-up 6-12 months How products hold up over time, warranty support experience

Testing Team

Our team includes:

  • 6'4", 250 lb - Tests weight capacity and size accommodations
  • 5'2", 110 lb - Tests adjustability for smaller users
  • Person with chronic back pain - Tests genuine ergonomic support
  • Video professional - Tests lighting and appearance on camera

Our Review Philosophy

No BS, No Sponsorships

We buy all products with our own money. We never accept free review units or sponsorship deals. If we recommend something, it's because we genuinely believe it's the best value for your money.

Transparent Testing

We document everything: unedited photos, real measurements, actual experiences. If a product has flaws, we'll tell you exactly what they are and whether they're deal-breakers.

Value-Focused

We're not looking for the cheapest option. We're looking for the best balance of price, quality, and features. Sometimes that means spending more on the right item.

Our Product Selection Criteria

Must-Have Criteria

  • Safety: Meets all relevant safety standards
  • Durability: Minimum 2-year expected lifespan with daily use
  • Warranty: At least 1-year manufacturer warranty
  • Availability: Readily available from multiple retailers
  • Return Policy: Reasonable return window (30+ days)

Nice-to-Have Criteria

  • Assembly: Under 60 minutes for one person
  • Adjustability: Multiple adjustment points
  • Aesthetics: Professional appearance
  • Sustainability: Recyclable materials or eco-friendly practices
